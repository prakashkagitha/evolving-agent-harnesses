§ 2 — The Nested Harness

One Bot, Five Roles

For every game turn the harness runs this pipeline. The fan-out to parallel specialists and the refute-until-converge refine loop are the "Dynamic Workflows" core. Two key safety properties are baked in: each specialist runs in its own base64-isolated namespace to prevent cross-contamination, and the referee enforces a non-suicidal floor that blocks any move into the bot's own body before applying the scoring policy.

§ 3 — The Evolutionary Loop

GEPA: Reflect, Mutate, Gate

Sonnet drives the outer loop. Each generation it reflects on the current population through one of four lenses, proposes a single-aspect mutation, re-runs the full harness, and accepts the edit only if the verified gate passes.

Honest Caveats

n = 1 seed. All results come from a single random seed (seed=0). The trajectory and contribution numbers are real, but their generalizability across seeds is unknown.

The structural gap is not evolution's work. The champion (61.7%) vs best-of-8 monolithic (21.4%) comparison reflects the harness scaffold making bots competent in the first place. Evolution's verified contribution is the 0.547 → 0.617 climb — meaningful (+7.0pp), but much smaller than the structural baseline advantage.

The "concept" lens was consistently destructive. All four concept mutations regressed (mean delta −0.224); the planner_prompt concept inventory was mostly stable despite direct selection pressure. This may reflect prompt sensitivity or inadequate search.

The same structural mutation failed three times before it worked. Adding combat was proposed in gens 1, 2, and 4 and rejected each time; it only passed in gen 3. Context (the lineage of the parent it was applied to) matters as much as the idea.

Fitness numbers at different sim counts differ. The in-evolution trajectory uses 120 sims (best-so-far gen4 = 0.607); the verified gate uses 200 sims (delta = +0.060 for the hero mutation); the final evaluation uses 1500 sims (champion = 0.617). All refer to the same bot g03_02; differences are sampling noise, not contradictions.
